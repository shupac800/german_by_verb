# FRAGEN

**FRAGEN:** to ask; to inquire; to question; to query

**ABFRAGEN** (sep.): to quiz; to test; to interrogate; to query; to poll; to scan

**ANFRAGEN** (sep.): to inquire; to request; to make a request; to apply for

**AUSFRAGEN** (sep.): to question; to interrogate; to pump for information; to grill; to debrief

**BEFRAGEN** (insep.): to question; to interrogate; to interview; to survey; to poll; to consult

**DURCHFRAGEN** (sep.): to ask one's way through; to get directions by asking

**ERFRAGEN** (insep.): to inquire; to find out by asking; to ascertain

**HINTERFRAGEN** (insep.): to question critically; to scrutinize; to examine; to challenge; to probe

**NACHFRAGEN** (sep.): to inquire further; to follow up; to ask again; *(economics)* to demand

**UMFRAGEN** (sep.): to poll; to survey; to canvass

**ÜBERFRAGEN** (insep.): to stump; to be beyond one's knowledge; to be unable to answer
