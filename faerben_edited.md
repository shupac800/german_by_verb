# FÄRBEN

**FÄRBEN:** to color; to dye; to tint; to stain; to tinge; to pigment

**ABFÄRBEN** (sep.): to rub off; to transfer color; *(figurative)* to rub off on; to influence

**DURCHFÄRBEN** (sep.): to dye through; to imbue; to saturate with color

**ENTFÄRBEN** (insep.): to decolorize; to bleach; to remove color from; to fade

**NACHFÄRBEN** (sep.): to re-dye; to touch up the color; to tint again

**VERFÄRBEN** (insep.): to discolor; to change color; to fade

**WIEDERFÄRBEN** (sep.): to re-dye; to color again
