# REIßEN

**REIßEN:** to tear; to rip; to rend; to jerk; to yank; to wrench

**ABREIßEN** (sep.): *(tear off)* to tear off; to rip off; *(demolish)* to tear down; to demolish; to pull down; *(break off)* to break off; to come to an end

**ANREIßEN** (sep.): *(tear)* to tear open slightly; to start; *(mark)* to scribe; to mark out

**AUFREIßEN** (sep.): to tear open; to fling open; to rip open; to burst open

**AUSREIßEN** (sep.): *(tear out)* to tear out; to pull out; to uproot; *(flee)* to run away; to escape; to bolt

**DURCHREIßEN** (sep.): to tear in two; to rip through

**EINREIßEN** (sep.): *(tear down)* to tear down; to pull down; *(spread)* to become established; to take hold; to set in

**ENTREIßEN** (insep.): to snatch; to wrest from; to wrench away

**HERAUSREIßEN** (sep.): to tear out; to rip out; to pull out; to save at the last moment

**HINREIßEN** (sep.): to carry away; to enrapture; to thrill; to captivate

**LOSREIßEN** (sep.): to tear free; to break free; to rip loose

**MITREIßEN** (sep.): *(drag)* to drag along; to sweep along; *(captivate)* to carry away; to captivate; to enthuse

**UMREIßEN** (sep./insep.): *(sep.)* to knock over; to knock down; *(insep.)* to outline; to sketch; to delineate

**VERREIßEN** (insep.): *(criticize)* to tear to pieces; to pan; to savage; *(ruin)* to botch; to bungle

**WEGREIßEN** (sep.): to tear away; to rip off; to pull away

**ZERREIßEN** (sep.): to tear up; to tear to pieces; to rip apart; to shred

**ZURÜCKREIßEN** (sep.): to jerk back; to yank back; to pull back

**ZUSAMMENREIßEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich zusammenreißen)* to pull oneself together; to get a grip
