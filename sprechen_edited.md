# SPRECHEN

**SPRECHEN:** to speak; to talk; to say; to converse

**ABSPRECHEN** (sep.): *(agree)* to agree on; to arrange; *(deny)* to deny; to dispute; to strip of

**ANSPRECHEN** (sep.): *(address)* to address; to speak to; to approach; *(appeal)* to appeal to; to attract; *(raise)* to bring up; to broach; to touch on

**AUSSPRECHEN** (sep.): *(pronounce)* to pronounce; to articulate; *(express)* to express; to voice; to declare; *(finish, reflexive)* to finish speaking

**BESPRECHEN** (insep.): to discuss; to talk over; to review

**DURCHSPRECHEN** (sep.): to discuss; to talk through

**ENTSPRECHEN** (insep.): to correspond to; to match; to meet; to comply with; to suit

**LOSSPRECHEN** (sep.): to absolve; to acquit; to exonerate

**NACHSPRECHEN** (sep.): to repeat; to say after

**VERSPRECHEN** (insep.): *(promise)* to promise; to pledge; *(misspeak, reflexive)* to misspeak; to make a slip of the tongue

**VORSPRECHEN** (sep.): *(recite)* to recite; to pronounce for someone; *(audition)* to audition

**WIDERSPRECHEN** (insep.): to contradict; to disagree with; to object to; to oppose

**ZUSPRECHEN** (sep.): to award; to grant; to adjudge
