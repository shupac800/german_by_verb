# GIESSEN

**GIESSEN:** to pour; to cast; to mold; *(garden)* to water; *(rain)* to pour down

**ABGIESSEN** (sep.): to pour off; to drain; to decant; to strain

**AUFGIESSEN** (sep.): to pour on; to infuse; to brew

**AUSGIESSEN** (sep.): to pour out; to empty out; to spill

**BEGIESSEN** (insep.): to water; to irrigate; to douse; to sprinkle; *(celebrate)* to drink to; to toast

**EINGIESSEN** (sep.): to pour in; to fill; to pour a drink

**ERGIESSEN** (insep.): to pour forth; to gush; to flow out; to discharge

**UMGIESSEN** (sep.): to pour into another container; to decant; to recast

**VERGIESSEN** (insep.): to spill; to shed; *(tears/blood)* to shed tears; to shed blood

**ZUGIESSEN** (sep.): to pour more; to add by pouring; to top up

**ÜBERGIESSEN** (insep.): to pour over; to douse; to drench
