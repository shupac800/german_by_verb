# SCHLAFEN

**SCHLAFEN:** to sleep; to slumber; to be asleep

**AUSSCHLAFEN** (sep.): to sleep in; to sleep one's fill; to get enough sleep

**BESCHLAFEN** (insep.): to sleep on; to think over overnight

**DURCHSCHLAFEN** (sep.): to sleep through; to sleep without waking

**EINSCHLAFEN** (sep.): *(fall asleep)* to fall asleep; to drop off; *(die down)* to peter out; to die down; *(go numb)* to go numb; to go to sleep

**ENTSCHLAFEN** (insep.): *(euphemism)* to pass away; to die

**VERSCHLAFEN** (insep.): to oversleep; to sleep through; to miss by sleeping — (adj.) sleepy; drowsy; dozy

**ÜBERSCHLAFEN** (insep.): to sleep on; to think over overnight
