# ZÄHLEN

**ZÄHLEN:** to count; to number; to enumerate; to reckon; to tally; to check off; to be included in; to take into account

**ABZÄHLEN** (sep.): to count; to count off; to add up; to sum up; to enumerate; to tell off

**AUFZÄHLEN** (sep.): to enumerate; to list; to name one by one; to count up; to recite

**AUSZÄHLEN** (sep.): to count out; to compute; to calculate; to reckon

**DURCHZÄHLEN** (sep.): to count through; to total; to number

**ERZÄHLEN** (insep.): to narrate; to tell; to relate; to recount; to describe

**MITZÄHLEN** (sep.): to count in; to include

**NACHZÄHLEN** (sep.): to recount; to check; to verify; to count again

**VERZÄHLEN** (sep.): to miscount; to count incorrectly; to miscalculate
