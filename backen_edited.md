# BACKEN

**BACKEN:** to bake; to cook in an oven

**AUSBACKEN** (sep.): to deep-fry; to cook in oil; to fry until done

**DURCHBACKEN** (sep.): to bake through; to bake thoroughly

**GEBACKEN** (adj.): baked; cooked by dry heat in an oven

**ÜBERBACKEN** (insep.): to gratinate; to top with melted cheese; to au gratin
