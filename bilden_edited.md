# BILDEN

**BILDEN:** *(form)* to form; to shape; to create; to constitute; to establish; *(educate)* to educate; to instruct; to cultivate

**ABBILDEN** (sep.): to depict; to portray; to image; to map; to represent; to picture

**AUSBILDEN** (sep.): to train; to educate; to instruct; to qualify; to school; to coach; to develop

**EINBILDEN** (sep.): to imagine; to fancy; to delude oneself; *(reflexive: sich einbilden)* to be conceited about

**FORTBILDEN** (sep.): to continue one's education; to develop further; to specialize

**NACHBILDEN** (sep.): to replicate; to copy; to reproduce; to imitate; to simulate; to re-create; to emulate

**UMBILDEN** (sep.): to reshuffle; to reorganize; to reshape; to remodel; to restructure; to metamorphose

**VERBILDEN** (insep.): to miseducate; to spoil through bad upbringing; to deform; to disfigure

**ZURÜCKBILDEN** (sep.): to regress; to recede; to atrophy; to shrink back; to involute
