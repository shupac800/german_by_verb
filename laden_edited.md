# LADEN

**LADEN:** to load; to charge; to lade; to burden

**ABLADEN** (sep.): to unload; to dump; to offload

**AUFLADEN** (sep.): to load; to load up; to charge; to recharge; to supercharge

**AUSLADEN** (sep.): *(unload)* to unload; to unpack; *(uninvite)* to disinvite; to uninvite

**BELADEN** (insep.): to load; to burden; to freight; to lade — (adj.) laden; loaded; burdened

**EINLADEN** (sep.): *(invite)* to invite; to ask; to bid; *(load)* to load; to load up

**ENTLADEN** (insep.): to discharge; to unload; *(reflexive: sich entladen)* to discharge; to erupt; to explode; to vent

**NACHLADEN** (sep.): to reload; to recharge; to refill

**UMLADEN** (sep.): to transship; to transfer; to reload

**VERLADEN** (insep.): to load; to ship; to dispatch; to put on board

**VORLADEN** (insep.): to summon; to subpoena; to cite

**ZULADEN** (sep.): to load additionally; to add to the load

**ZURÜCKLADEN** (sep.): to reload; to load back

**ÜBERLADEN** (insep.): to overload; to overburden; to overcharge — (adj.) overloaded; overladen; overblown; ornate
