# LERNEN

**LERNEN:** to learn; to study; to acquire knowledge

**ANLERNEN** (sep.): to train; to teach on the job; to instruct

**AUSLERNEN** (sep.): to finish one's training; to complete an apprenticeship

**ERLERNEN** (insep.): to learn; to acquire; to master

**UMLERNEN** (sep.): to retrain; to relearn; to rethink; to adjust one's thinking

**VERLERNEN** (insep.): to forget; to unlearn; to lose the ability
