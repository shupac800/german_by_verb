# DIENEN

**DIENEN:** to serve; to be of use; to be useful; to function as; to have a purpose; to attend

**ABDIENEN** (sep.): to serve out; to complete one's term of service

**AUSDIENEN** (sep.): to finish one's service; to complete one's military service; to be discharged

**BEDIENEN** (insep.): *(person)* to serve; to wait on; to attend to; *(machine)* to operate; to work; to handle

**VERDIENEN** (insep.): *(earn)* to earn; to make; to bring in; *(deserve)* to deserve; to merit; to be worthy of
