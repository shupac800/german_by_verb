# BRENNEN

**BRENNEN:** to burn; to be on fire; to blaze; to flame; to sting; to scorch; to sear; *(distill)* to distill

**ABBRENNEN** (sep.): to burn down; to burn to the ground; to burn off; *(fireworks)* to set off; to let off

**ANBRENNEN** (sep.): to catch fire; to start burning; *(cooking)* to burn; to scorch; to singe

**AUSBRENNEN** (sep.): to burn out; to gut by fire; to cauterize; to be extinguished

**DURCHBRENNEN** (sep.): *(electrical)* to blow; to burn out; *(colloquial)* to run away; to elope; to abscond

**EINBRENNEN** (sep.): to brand; to burn in; to sear into; to engrave by burning

**ENTBRENNEN** (insep.): to flare up; to erupt; to break out; to kindle; to ignite

**VERBRENNEN** (insep.): to burn; to burn up; to incinerate; to cremate; to scorch; to scald; to combust

**WEGBRENNEN** (sep.): to burn away; to burn off

**ÜBERBRENNEN** (insep.): to overburn; to overbake; to overfire
