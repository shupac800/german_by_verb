# HÖREN

**HÖREN:** to hear; to listen; to perceive by ear; to obey; to listen to

**ABHÖREN** (sep.): to wiretap; to eavesdrop on; to tap; to bug; to listen in on; *(test)* to quiz; to test orally

**ANHÖREN** (sep.): to listen to; to hear; to give a hearing to; *(reflexive: sich anhören)* to sound; to seem

**AUFHÖREN** (sep.): to stop; to cease; to quit; to discontinue; to desist; to leave off; to break off

**ERHÖREN** (insep.): to hear; to answer; to grant; to heed a prayer or plea

**GEHÖREN** (insep.): to belong to; to be the property of; to be part of; to pertain to; to be appropriate

**HERAUSHÖREN** (sep.): to detect by listening; to discern; to pick up on; to hear in someone's voice

**HERHÖREN** (sep.): to listen; to pay attention; to listen here

**HINHÖREN** (sep.): to listen; to listen carefully; to pay attention

**MITHÖREN** (sep.): to listen in; to overhear; to eavesdrop; to monitor

**UMHÖREN** (sep.): to ask around; to inquire around; to listen around for information

**VERHÖREN** (insep.): *(interrogate)* to interrogate; to question; to examine; *(mishear, reflexive)* to mishear; to hear incorrectly

**ZUHÖREN** (sep.): to listen; to listen to; to pay attention; to hear out; to give ear to

**ÜBERHÖREN** (insep.): to fail to hear; to not hear; to ignore; to pretend not to hear
