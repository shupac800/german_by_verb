# BETEN

**BETEN:** to pray; to worship

**ANBETEN** (sep.): to worship; to adore; to idolize; to venerate; to revere; to glorify; to exalt; to deify; to dote on

**ERBETEN** (insep.): to request; to solicit; to call upon

**GEBETEN** (adj.): requested; asked for; invited

**HERBETEN** (sep.): to rattle off; to recite mechanically

**NACHBETEN** (sep.): to repeat uncritically; to parrot; to echo without thinking

**VORBETEN** (sep.): to lead in prayer; to recite a prayer for others to follow
