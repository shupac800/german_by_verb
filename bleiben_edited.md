# BLEIBEN

**BLEIBEN:** to stay; to remain; to continue; to abide; to keep; to be left

**AUFBLEIBEN** (sep.): to stay up; to stay awake; to sit up; to wait up

**AUSBLEIBEN** (sep.): to fail to appear; to be absent; to stay away; to not materialize

**DABEIBLEIBEN** (sep.): to stick with; to stay with; to keep at

**DABLEIBEN** (sep.): to stay; to remain; to stay put

**GLEICHBLEIBEN** (sep.): to remain the same; to remain constant; to stay unchanged

**NACHBLEIBEN** (sep.): to remain behind; to be left over; to stay on

**UNTERBLEIBEN** (insep.): to not take place; to remain undone; to be omitted; to be left off

**VERBLEIBEN** (insep.): to remain; to stay; to be left; to stay put

**WEGBLEIBEN** (sep.): to stay away; to keep away; to fail to show up

**ZUBLEIBEN** (sep.): to stay closed; to stay shut; to remain shut

**ZURÜCKBLEIBEN** (sep.): to fall behind; to lag behind; to be left behind; to stay behind; to trail; to straggle

**ÜBERBLEIBEN** (sep.): to be left over; to remain; to survive
