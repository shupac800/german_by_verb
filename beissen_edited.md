# BEISSEN

**BEISSEN:** to bite; to grip with the teeth; to take bait; to sting; to nibble

**ABBEISSEN** (sep.): to bite off; to cut off with the teeth

**ANBEISSEN** (sep.): to bite into; to take a bite of; to take the bait

**AUSBEISSEN** (sep.): to sweat over a problem; to struggle with a difficulty

**DURCHBEISSEN** (sep.): to bite through; to plod on; to persevere; to tough it out

**VERBEISSEN** (insep.): to suppress; to repress; to bottle up; to hold in; to stifle

**ZERBEISSEN** (sep.): to chew; to chew up; to masticate; to crush with the teeth

**ZUBEISSEN** (sep.): to bite; to snap; to clamp down with the teeth

**ZUSAMMENBEISSEN** (sep.): to grit; to clench one's teeth; to grind the teeth together
