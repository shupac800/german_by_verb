# SCHMEIßEN

**SCHMEIßEN:** to throw; to hurl; to fling; to chuck; to toss

**HERAUSSCHMEIßEN** (sep.): to throw out; to kick out; to chuck out

**HINSCHMEIßEN** (sep.): *(throw down)* to fling down; to chuck down; *(give up)* to chuck it in; to throw in the towel

**WEGSCHMEIßEN** (sep.): to throw away; to discard; to chuck out

**ZERSCHMEIßEN** (sep.): to smash; to shatter; to break to pieces

**ZUSCHMEIßEN** (sep.): to slam shut; to bang shut
