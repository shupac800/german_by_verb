# FOLGEN

**FOLGEN:** to follow; to go after; to ensue; to result from; to succeed; to obey; to comply

**BEFOLGEN** (insep.): to obey; to comply with; to follow; to observe; to adhere to

**ERFOLGEN** (insep.): to ensue; to occur; to take place; to happen; to follow as a result

**NACHFOLGEN** (sep.): to follow; to succeed; to come after; to take over from; to emulate

**VERFOLGEN** (insep.): to pursue; to chase; to track; to trail; to hunt; *(persecute)* to persecute; to victimize; *(monitor)* to follow; to keep track of
