# LEBEN

**LEBEN:** to live; to be alive; to exist; to reside; to dwell

**ABLEBEN** (sep.): to die; to pass away; to decease

**AUFLEBEN** (sep.): to revive; to come alive; to perk up; to brighten up

**AUSLEBEN** (sep.): to live out; to express fully; to indulge

**BELEBEN** (insep.): to animate; to enliven; to invigorate; to revitalize; to stimulate; to liven up

**DURCHLEBEN** (insep.): to live through; to experience; to go through; to endure

**EINLEBEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich einleben)* to settle in; to get accustomed; to adjust

**ERLEBEN** (insep.): to experience; to live through; to witness; to live to see

**FORTLEBEN** (sep.): to live on; to survive; to endure; to continue to exist

**ZUSAMMENLEBEN** (sep.): to live together; to cohabit; to coexist

**ÜBERLEBEN** (insep.): to survive; to outlive; to outlast; to endure
