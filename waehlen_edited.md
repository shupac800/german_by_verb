# WÄHLEN

**WÄHLEN:** to choose; to select; to pick; to opt for; *(telephone)* to dial; *(politics)* to elect; to vote for; to cast a ballot

**ABWÄHLEN** (sep.): to vote out; to vote out of office; to deselect

**AUSWÄHLEN** (sep.): to choose; to select; to pick out; to single out; to handpick; to screen

**ERWÄHLEN** (insep.): to choose; to elect; to select

**HINZUWÄHLEN** (sep.): to co-opt; to add by election

**VERWÄHLEN** (sep.): sich verwählen — to misdial; to dial the wrong number

**VORWÄHLEN** (sep.): to preselect; to predial

**WIEDERWÄHLEN** (sep.): *(politics)* to reelect; to elect again; *(telephone)* to redial
