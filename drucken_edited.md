# DRUCKEN

**DRUCKEN:** to print; to imprint; to press ink onto paper; to publish

**ABDRUCKEN** (sep.): to print; to reprint; to publish; to reproduce in print

**ANDRUCKEN** (sep.): to proof; to make a trial print; to pull a proof

**AUFDRUCKEN** (sep.): to print on; to imprint; to stamp onto a surface

**AUSDRUCKEN** (sep.): to print out; to produce a printed copy

**BEDRUCKEN** (insep.): to print on; to print a design or pattern onto

**EINDRUCKEN** (sep.): to impress; to stamp; to press a mark into

**NACHDRUCKEN** (sep.): to reprint; to print again; to reproduce

**VERDRUCKEN** (insep.): to misprint; to make a typographical error
