# LAUTEN

**LAUTEN:** to read; to go; to run; to be worded; to state

**ABLAUTEN** (sep.): *(linguistics)* to undergo ablaut; to change vowel by ablaut

**ANLAUTEN** (sep.): *(linguistics)* to begin with a sound; to have as an initial sound

**AUSLAUTEN** (sep.): *(linguistics)* to end in a sound; to have as a final sound

**VERLAUTEN** (insep.): to be reported; to become known; to be disclosed
