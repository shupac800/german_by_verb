# KAUEN

**KAUEN:** to chew; to masticate; to chomp; to gnaw

**ABKAUEN** (sep.): to chew off; to bite off; to gnaw off

**DURCHKAUEN** (sep.): *(literal)* to chew thoroughly; *(figurative)* to go over in detail; to chew over

**VORKAUEN** (sep.): *(literal)* to chew for someone; *(figurative)* to spell out; to spoon-feed; to explain in painstaking detail

**ZERKAUEN** (sep.): to chew up; to chew to pieces; to masticate thoroughly
