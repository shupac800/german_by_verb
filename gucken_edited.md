# GUCKEN

**GUCKEN:** to look; to peek; to glance; to gaze; to watch; to peer; to peep

**ABGUCKEN** (sep.): to copy; to crib; to imitate; to peek at someone's work

**ANGUCKEN** (sep.): to look at; to watch; to eye; to observe

**AUSGUCKEN** (sep.): to look out for; to seek; to scan for

**BEGUCKEN** (insep.): to look at; to have a look at; to inspect

**DURCHGUCKEN** (sep.): to look through; to peep through; to peer through

**HERAUSGUCKEN** (sep.): to look out; to peek out; to appear; to show

**HINGUCKEN** (sep.): to look; to glance; to watch; to pay attention

**HINWEGGUCKEN** (sep.): to overlook; to look past; to ignore; to disregard
