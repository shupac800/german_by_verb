# SEHEN

**SEHEN:** to see; to look; to watch; to perceive; to behold

**ABSEHEN** (sep.): *(foresee)* to foresee; to predict; *(refrain)* to refrain from; to disregard

**ANSEHEN** (sep.): *(look at)* to look at; to watch; to view; *(regard)* to regard; to consider; to view as

**AUFSEHEN** (sep.): to look up

**AUSSEHEN** (sep.): to look; to appear; to seem

**BESEHEN** (insep.): to look at; to inspect; to examine

**DURCHSEHEN** (sep.): *(look through)* to look through; *(review)* to go through; to check; to review

**EINSEHEN** (sep.): *(understand)* to understand; to realize; to recognize; *(inspect)* to look at; to inspect

**ENTGEGENSEHEN** (sep.): to look forward to; to expect; to await

**ERSEHEN** (insep.): to see from; to gather from; to be evident from

**GLEICHSEHEN** (sep.): to resemble; to look like

**HERAUSSEHEN** (sep.): to look out; to peek out

**HERSEHEN** (sep.): to look here; to look this way

**HINSEHEN** (sep.): to look; to look there; to watch

**HINWEGSEHEN** (sep.): to overlook; to disregard; to turn a blind eye to

**NACHSEHEN** (sep.): *(look up)* to look up; to check; *(overlook)* to overlook; to let pass; *(look after)* to look after; to watch

**UMSEHEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich umsehen)* to look around; to look about

**VERSEHEN** (insep.): *(carry out)* to carry out; to perform; to attend to; *(provide)* to provide; to supply; to equip; *(err, reflexive)* to make a mistake; to err

**VORSEHEN** (sep./insep.): *(insep.)* to plan; to provide for; to intend; to schedule; *(sep., reflexive)* to watch out; to be careful

**WEGSEHEN** (sep.): to look away; to avert one's eyes

**WIEDERSEHEN** (sep.): to see again; to meet again

**ZURÜCKSEHEN** (sep.): to look back

**ZUSEHEN** (sep.): to watch; to look on; to make sure

**ÜBERSEHEN** (insep.): *(survey)* to survey; to take in; *(miss)* to overlook; to miss; to fail to notice
