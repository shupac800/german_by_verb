# KAUEN

**ABKAUEN:** to chew; to bite

**DURCHKAUEN:** to chew carefully; to chew thoroughly

**VORKAUEN:** to chew out; to spell out; to read slowly letter by letter; to make clear; to explain in detail; to chew for

**ZERKAUEN:** to chew; to grind food with the teeth
