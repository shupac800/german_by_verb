# HOLEN

**AUFHOLEN** (sep.): to catch up; to make up ground; to close the gap; to make up for lost time

**AUSHOLEN** (sep.): to take a swing; to draw back; to wind up; *(figurative)* to go into detail; to launch into a long explanation

**EINHOLEN** (sep.): *(catch up)* to catch up with; to overtake; *(obtain)* to obtain; to get; to seek; *(take down)* to haul in; to take down; to lower

**ERHOLEN** (insep.): *(reflexive: sich erholen)* to recover; to recuperate; to rest; to relax; to rebound

**HERAUFHOLEN** (sep.): to bring up; to fetch up; to haul up

**HERAUSHOLEN** (sep.): to get out; to bring out; to extract; to squeeze out; to gain

**HERHOLEN** (sep.): to fetch; to go and get; to bring here

**NACHHOLEN** (sep.): to catch up on; to make up for; to do belatedly; to fetch later

**WEGHOLEN** (sep.): to take away; to fetch away; to remove

**WIEDERHOLEN** (insep.): to repeat; to reiterate; to do again; to replay; to review; to recap

**ZURÜCKHOLEN** (sep.): to fetch back; to bring back; to retrieve; to get back
