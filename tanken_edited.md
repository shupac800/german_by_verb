# TANKEN

**TANKEN:** to fuel; to refuel; to fill up with fuel; to gas up

**AUFTANKEN** (sep.): to refuel; to fill up with fuel; to replenish fuel

**BETANKEN** (insep.): to fuel; to fill with fuel

**NACHTANKEN** (sep.): to refuel; to top up the tank

**VOLLTANKEN** (sep.): to fill up; to fill the tank completely

**ZUTANKEN** (sep.): to refuel; to add more fuel
