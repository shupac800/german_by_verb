# BIETEN

**BIETEN:** to offer; to present; to provide; to propose; to put forward; to submit; to hold out

**ANBIETEN** (sep.): to offer; to propose; to tender; to proffer; to volunteer; to suggest; to make available

**AUFBIETEN** (sep.): to muster; to summon; to call up; to exert; to marshal

**AUSBIETEN** (sep.): to offer; to bid; to tender; to put up for sale

**ENTBIETEN** (insep.): to convey; to extend; to express; to offer formally

**ERBIETEN** (insep.): to volunteer; to offer one's services; to offer to do

**GEBIETEN** (insep.): to command; to order; to demand; to bid; to decree; to enjoin; to dictate

**UNTERBIETEN** (insep.): to underbid; to undercut; to undersell; to offer a lower price than

**VERBIETEN** (insep.): to forbid; to prohibit; to ban; to proscribe; to interdict; to outlaw

**ÜBERBIETEN** (insep.): to outbid; to surpass; to exceed; to outdo; to excel; to top
