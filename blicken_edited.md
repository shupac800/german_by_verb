# BLICKEN

**BLICKEN:** to look; to gaze; to glance; to see; to observe

**ANBLICKEN** (sep.): to look at; to gaze at; to regard; to stare at

**AUFBLICKEN** (sep.): to look up; to raise one's eyes; to look up to; to admire

**AUSBLICKEN** (sep.): to look out; to observe; to gaze outward

**DURCHBLICKEN** (sep.): to look through; to see through; *(figurative)* to understand; to comprehend; to grasp

**EMPORBLICKEN** (sep.): to look up; to raise one's eyes; to gaze upward

**ERBLICKEN** (insep.): to behold; to catch sight of; to spot; to perceive; to notice; to sight

**NACHBLICKEN** (sep.): to look after; to gaze after; to watch someone go

**WEGBLICKEN** (sep.): to look away; to avert one's gaze

**ZURÜCKBLICKEN** (sep.): to look back; to look behind; to glance back; to reflect on the past

**ÜBERBLICKEN** (insep.): to survey; to overlook; to take in; to review; to have an overview of
