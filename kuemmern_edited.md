# KÜMMERN

**KÜMMERN:** *(reflexive: sich kümmern)* to take care of; to look after; to attend to; to see to; *(concern)* to concern; to trouble; to worry

**BEKÜMMERN** (insep.): to distress; to grieve; to trouble; to sadden; to cause sorrow

**VERKÜMMERN** (insep.): to wither; to atrophy; to waste away; to decline; to wilt
