# DEUTEN

**DEUTEN:** to interpret; to explain; to indicate; to point; to portend

**ANDEUTEN** (sep.): to hint at; to allude to; to suggest; to imply; to intimate; to indicate; to foreshadow

**AUSDEUTEN** (sep.): to interpret; to explain; to elucidate; to read into

**BEDEUTEN** (insep.): to mean; to signify; to indicate; to imply; to stand for; to symbolize; to connote

**HINDEUTEN** (sep.): to point to; to indicate; to suggest; to allude to; to portend

**MISSDEUTEN** (insep.): to misinterpret; to misconstrue; to misread; to understand incorrectly
