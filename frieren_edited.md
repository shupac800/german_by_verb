# FRIEREN

**FRIEREN:** to freeze; to be cold; to feel cold; to suffer from cold

**ABFRIEREN** (sep.): to get frostbitten; to freeze off

**ANFRIEREN** (sep.): to freeze on; to freeze to; to stick by freezing

**DURCHFRIEREN** (sep.): to be chilled through; to be frozen to the bone

**EINFRIEREN** (sep.): to freeze; to freeze solid; *(figurative)* to freeze; to suspend; to block

**ERFRIEREN** (insep.): to freeze to death; to die of cold; to be killed by frost

**GEFRIEREN** (insep.): to freeze; to solidify; to congeal; to turn to ice

**VERFRIEREN** (insep.): to be chilled through; to get thoroughly cold

**ZUFRIEREN** (sep.): to freeze over; to ice over; to become covered with ice

**ÜBERFRIEREN** (insep.): to freeze over; to glaze over with ice
