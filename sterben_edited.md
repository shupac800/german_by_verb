# STERBEN

**STERBEN:** to die; to pass away; to perish; to expire

**ABSTERBEN** (sep.): to die off; to wither; to go numb; to cease functioning

**AUSSTERBEN** (sep.): to die out; to become extinct

**ERSTERBEN** (insep.): to die away; to fade; to trail off; to fall silent

**HINSTERBEN** (sep.): to pass away; to die

**VERSTERBEN** (insep.): to pass away; to die; to decease
