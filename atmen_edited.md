# ATMEN

**ATMEN:** to breathe; to respire; to inhale and exhale; to take a breath

**AUFATMEN** (sep.): to breathe a sigh of relief; to breathe freely again

**AUSATMEN** (sep.): to exhale; to breathe out; to blow out

**BEATMEN** (insep.): to ventilate; to supply artificial respiration; to resuscitate

**DURCHATMEN** (sep.): to take a deep breath; to breathe deeply

**EINATMEN** (sep.): to inhale; to breathe in; to sniff; to take in air
