# SCHAFFEN

**SCHAFFEN:** *(create)* to create; to produce; to make; *(manage)* to manage; to accomplish; to handle; *(work)* to work; to labor

**ABSCHAFFEN** (sep.): to abolish; to do away with; to get rid of; to eliminate

**ANSCHAFFEN** (sep.): *(acquire)* to acquire; to buy; to purchase; *(colloquial)* to prostitute oneself

**AUSSCHAFFEN** (sep.): to accomplish; to complete

**BESCHAFFEN** (insep.): to obtain; to procure; to provide; to get hold of

**ERSCHAFFEN** (insep.): to create; to bring into being

**FORTSCHAFFEN** (sep.): to remove; to take away; to cart off

**HERAUSSCHAFFEN** (sep.): to get out; to remove

**HERSCHAFFEN** (sep.): to bring here; to get here

**HINSCHAFFEN** (sep.): to get there; to transport there

**VERSCHAFFEN** (insep.): to obtain; to procure; to provide; to get; to secure
