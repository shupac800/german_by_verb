# REDEN

**REDEN:** to talk; to speak; to converse; to discuss

**ABREDEN** (sep.): to dissuade; to talk out of

**ANREDEN** (sep.): to address; to speak to

**AUSREDEN** (sep.): *(dissuade)* to talk out of; to dissuade from; *(finish speaking)* to finish speaking; to let finish

**BEREDEN** (insep.): to discuss; to talk over; to debate

**EINREDEN** (sep.): to persuade; to talk into; to put into someone's head

**HERAUSREDEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich herausreden)* to talk one's way out of; to make excuses

**HERREDEN** (sep.): to talk nonsense; to ramble

**MITREDEN** (sep.): to join in the conversation; to have a say

**NACHREDEN** (sep.): *(repeat)* to repeat; *(gossip)* to gossip about; to say behind someone's back

**ZERREDEN** (sep.): to talk to death; to overanalyze through discussion

**ZUREDEN** (sep.): to encourage; to urge; to persuade; to coax

**ÜBERREDEN** (insep.): to persuade; to talk into; to convince; to win over
