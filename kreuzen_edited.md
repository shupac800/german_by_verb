# KREUZEN

**KREUZEN:** *(intersect)* to cross; to intersect; *(biology)* to crossbreed; to hybridize; *(nautical)* to cruise; to tack; *(religious)* to make the sign of the cross

**ANKREUZEN** (sep.): to mark with a cross; to tick; to check off

**AUFKREUZEN** (sep.): to show up; to turn up; to appear unexpectedly

**BEKREUZEN** (insep.): *(reflexive: sich bekreuzen)* to cross oneself; to make the sign of the cross

**DURCHKREUZEN** (insep.): to thwart; to foil; to frustrate; to cross

**ÜBERKREUZEN** (insep.): *(reflexive: sich überkreuzen)* to cross; to intersect; to overlap
