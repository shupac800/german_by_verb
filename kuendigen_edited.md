# KÜNDIGEN

**KÜNDIGEN:** to give notice; to terminate; to cancel; to quit; to dismiss; to fire

**ANKÜNDIGEN** (sep.): to announce; to herald; to advertise; to proclaim; to give notice of

**AUFKÜNDIGEN** (sep.): to terminate; to renounce; to revoke; to cancel

**VERKÜNDIGEN** (insep.): to proclaim; to announce; to declare; to preach; to promulgate
