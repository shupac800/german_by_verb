# WACHSEN

**WACHSEN:** to grow; to increase; to rise; to expand; to sprout; to wax

**ANWACHSEN** (sep.): to grow; to increase; to expand; to swell

**AUFWACHSEN** (sep.): to grow up; to mature; to be raised

**BEWACHSEN** (insep.): to grow over; to cover with vegetation — (adj.) overgrown; covered with vegetation

**DURCHWACHSEN** (sep.): to grow through; to permeate with growth — (adj.) *(meat)* streaky; marbled; *(colloquial)* mixed; so-so; mediocre

**EINWACHSEN** (sep.): *(grow)* to grow in; to become ingrown; *(polish)* to wax; to polish with wax

**EMPORWACHSEN** (sep.): to grow upward; to shoot up; to rise

**ENTWACHSEN** (insep.): to outgrow; to grow out of

**ERWACHSEN** (adj.): adult; mature; grown-up

**GEWACHSEN** (adj.): equal to; up to; a match for

**HERAUSWACHSEN** (sep.): to outgrow; to grow out of; to become too big for

**NACHWACHSEN** (sep.): to grow back; to regrow; to regenerate

**UMWACHSEN** (insep.): to grow around; to overgrow; to enclose with growth

**UNTERWACHSEN** (adj.): undergrown; sparse in undergrowth

**VERWACHSEN** (insep.): *(heal)* to heal; to close; to knit; to grow together — (adj.) deformed; misshapen; gnarled; overgrown; ingrown

**ZUSAMMENWACHSEN** (sep.): to grow together; to knit; to heal; to fuse; to accrete

**ZUWACHSEN** (sep.): *(heal)* to heal over; to close up; *(overgrow)* to become overgrown; *(increase)* to accrue; to increase

**ÜBERWACHSEN** (insep.): to overgrow; to cover with growth; to overrun
