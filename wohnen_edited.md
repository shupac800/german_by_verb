# WOHNEN

**WOHNEN:** to live; to reside; to dwell; to lodge

**BEIWOHNEN** (insep.): to attend; to be present at; to accompany

**BEWOHNEN** (insep.): to inhabit; to occupy; to reside in; to dwell in; to live in

**VERWOHNEN** (insep.): to wear out through long residence

**ZUSAMMENWOHNEN** (sep.): to cohabit; to live together; to share a home
