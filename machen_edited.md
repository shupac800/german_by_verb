# MACHEN

**MACHEN:** to make; to do; to cause; to produce; to create

**ABMACHEN** (sep.): *(agree)* to agree on; to arrange; to settle; *(remove)* to take off; to remove; to detach

**ANMACHEN** (sep.): *(turn on)* to turn on; to switch on; *(light)* to light; to start; *(food)* to dress; to season; *(flirt, colloquial)* to chat up; to hit on

**AUFMACHEN** (sep.): to open; to open up; to undo; to unfasten

**AUSMACHEN** (sep.): *(turn off)* to turn off; to switch off; to put out; *(agree)* to agree on; to arrange; *(constitute)* to constitute; to amount to; to make up; *(matter)* to matter; to bother

**DARANMACHEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich daranmachen)* to set about; to get down to; to get started on

**DURCHMACHEN** (sep.): to go through; to experience; to endure; to suffer through

**EINMACHEN** (sep.): to preserve; to can; to bottle; to put up

**FESTMACHEN** (sep.): *(secure)* to fasten; to secure; to tie; to attach; *(nautical)* to moor; to make fast; *(determine)* to pin down; to fix; to nail down

**FORTMACHEN** (sep.): to get away; to leave; to hurry off

**GLEICHMACHEN** (sep.): to equalize; to make equal; to level

**HERAUSMACHEN** (sep.): to take out; to remove; *(reflexive: sich herausmachen)* to come along well; to do well

**HERMACHEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich hermachen über)* to set upon; to fall upon; to dig into

**LOSMACHEN** (sep.): to unfasten; to untie; to release; to loosen; to detach

**MITMACHEN** (sep.): to join in; to take part in; to participate; to go along with

**NACHMACHEN** (sep.): to imitate; to copy; to mimic; to emulate; to reproduce

**VERMACHEN** (insep.): to bequeath; to leave in a will; to leave to

**VORMACHEN** (sep.): *(demonstrate)* to demonstrate; to show how; *(deceive)* to fool; to kid; to delude

**WEGMACHEN** (sep.): to get rid of; to remove; to make disappear

**ZUMACHEN** (sep.): to close; to shut; to seal; to shut down
