# MÜSSEN

**MÜSSEN:** to must; to have to; to need to; to be obliged to

**DURCHMÜSSEN** (sep.): to have to go through; to have to get through

**FORTMÜSSEN** (sep.): to have to leave; to have to go away

**HERMÜSSEN** (sep.): to have to come here

**WEGMÜSSEN** (sep.): to have to go; to have to leave

**ZURÜCKMÜSSEN** (sep.): to have to go back; to have to return
