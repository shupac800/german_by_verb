# REISEN

**REISEN:** to travel; to journey; to go on a trip

**ABREISEN** (sep.): to depart; to leave; to set off

**ANREISEN** (sep.): to travel to; to arrive; to make one's way

**AUSREISEN** (sep.): to leave the country; to depart; to emigrate

**BEREISEN** (insep.): to travel through; to tour; to visit

**DURCHREISEN** (sep./insep.): to travel through; to pass through

**EINREISEN** (sep.): to enter the country; to arrive

**HERREISEN** (sep.): to travel here

**HINREISEN** (sep.): to travel there

**MITREISEN** (sep.): to travel along; to travel with

**NACHREISEN** (sep.): to travel after; to follow

**VERREISEN** (insep.): to go on a trip; to travel; to go away
