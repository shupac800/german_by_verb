# SINKEN

**SINKEN:** to sink; to descend; to drop; to fall; to decline; to diminish

**ABSINKEN** (sep.): to sink; to drop; to fall; to decline

**EINSINKEN** (sep.): to sink in; to cave in; to give way

**HINSINKEN** (sep.): to sink down; to collapse; to drop

**VERSINKEN** (insep.): to sink; to submerge; to be engulfed; to become absorbed

**ZUSAMMENSINKEN** (sep.): to collapse; to sink down; to crumple
