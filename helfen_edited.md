# HELFEN

**HELFEN:** to help; to assist; to aid; to be of use; to avail; to support

**ABHELFEN** (sep.): to remedy; to fix; to correct; to redress; to put right

**AUFHELFEN** (sep.): to help up; to assist in getting up; to give a hand up

**AUSHELFEN** (sep.): to help out; to lend a hand; to fill in; to stand in

**BEHELFEN** (insep.): *(reflexive: sich behelfen)* to make do; to manage; to get by; to improvise

**DURCHHELFEN** (sep.): to help through; to get someone through; to see someone through

**HERAUSHELFEN** (sep.): to help out of; to extricate; to get someone out of a difficulty

**HINWEGHELFEN** (sep.): to help over; to help get past; to see through

**MITHELFEN** (sep.): to help; to lend a hand; to pitch in; to contribute

**NACHHELFEN** (sep.): to give a helping hand; to assist; to give a nudge; to help along

**VERHELFEN** (insep.): to help to obtain; to help achieve; to enable; to assist in getting

**WEGHELFEN** (sep.): to help away; to help someone get away
