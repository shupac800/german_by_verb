# GLEICHEN

**GLEICHEN:** to equal; to resemble; to match; to be similar to; to correspond to

**ABGLEICHEN** (sep.): to balance; to reconcile; to calibrate; to synchronize; to coordinate

**ANGLEICHEN** (sep.): to assimilate; to adapt; to adjust; to conform; to align; to equalize; to match

**AUSGLEICHEN** (sep.): to balance; to compensate; to offset; to equalize; to counterbalance; to make up for; to settle

**BEGLEICHEN** (insep.): to pay; to settle; to discharge; to clear a debt

**VERGLEICHEN** (insep.): to compare; to contrast; to liken; to collate
