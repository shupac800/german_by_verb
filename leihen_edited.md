# LEIHEN

**LEIHEN:** to lend; to loan; to borrow; to hire

**AUSLEIHEN** (sep.): *(borrow)* to borrow; to check out; *(lend)* to lend; to loan out

**BELEIHEN** (insep.): to grant a loan on; to lend against; to mortgage

**ENTLEIHEN** (insep.): to borrow; to take on loan

**VERLEIHEN** (insep.): *(lend)* to lend; to loan; to rent out; to hire out; *(award)* to award; to confer; to bestow; to grant
