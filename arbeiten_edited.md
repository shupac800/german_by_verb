**DURCHARBEITEN** (sep.): to work through; to go through; to work late; to work overtime

**EINARBEITEN** (sep.): to train; to break in; to get accustomed to a job; to work in; to incorporate

**EMPORARBEITEN** (sep.): to work one's way up

**ENTGEGENARBEITEN** (sep.): to counteract; to work against; to oppose; to stonewall; to counter

**ERARBEITEN** (insep.): to work out; to elaborate; to devise; to acquire through effort

**HERAUSARBEITEN** (sep.): to carve out; to bring out; to shape; to express; to make evident

**UMARBEITEN** (sep.): to rework; to revamp; to make over; to remodel

**VERARBEITEN** (insep.): *(material)* to process; to work up; to use; to consume; *(food)* to digest; to assimilate; *(emotion)* to come to terms with; to cope with; to handle

**ZUSAMMENARBEITEN** (sep.): to collaborate; to cooperate; to work together; to liaise; to act in concert

**ÜBERARBEITEN** (insep.): *(overwork)* to overwork; to overexert; *(revise)* to revise; to edit; to overhaul; to rewrite; to copyedit; to rework
