# SCHAUEN

**SCHAUEN:** to look; to gaze; to watch; to see

**ANSCHAUEN** (sep.): to look at; to watch; to observe; to regard

**AUFSCHAUEN** (sep.): to look up; to raise one's eyes

**AUSSCHAUEN** (sep.): *(look out)* to look out; to watch out; *(appear)* to look; to appear

**BESCHAUEN** (insep.): to inspect; to examine; to contemplate; to look at closely

**DURCHSCHAUEN** (sep./insep.): *(sep.)* to look through; *(insep.)* to see through; to understand; to figure out

**EMPORSCHAUEN** (sep.): to look up; to raise one's eyes

**ERSCHAUEN** (insep.): to behold; to glimpse; to perceive

**HERAUSSCHAUEN** (sep.): to look out; to peek out; to stick out

**HINSCHAUEN** (sep.): to look; to watch; to look over there

**NACHSCHAUEN** (sep.): to look up; to check; to look after

**UMSCHAUEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich umschauen)* to look around; to look about

**ZUSCHAUEN** (sep.): to watch; to look on; to observe

**ÜBERSCHAUEN** (insep.): to survey; to overlook; to take in at a glance
