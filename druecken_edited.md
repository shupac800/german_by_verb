# DRÜCKEN

**DRÜCKEN:** to press; to push; to squeeze; to apply pressure; to depress; to weigh down; *(embrace)* to clutch; to hold tight; *(avoid)* to shirk

**ABDRÜCKEN** (sep.): *(firearm)* to fire; to pull the trigger; to discharge; *(imprint)* to make an impression of; to mold

**ANDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to press on; to press down; to press against

**AUFDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to press open; to squeeze out; to force open by pressing

**AUSDRÜCKEN** (sep.): *(express)* to express; to articulate; to formulate; to put into words; to voice; *(squeeze)* to squeeze out; to wring out; *(extinguish)* to stub out; to put out

**BEDRÜCKEN** (insep.): to distress; to oppress; to weigh down; to trouble; to depress; to burden; to sadden

**DURCHDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to push through; to press through; to force through

**EINDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to press in; to push in; to crush inward; to dent

**ERDRÜCKEN** (insep.): to crush; to overwhelm; to smother; to suffocate under pressure

**LOSDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to pull the trigger; to fire; to let off

**UNTERDRÜCKEN** (insep.): to suppress; to oppress; to repress; to stifle; to quash; to subdue; to put down; to hold back

**VERDRÜCKEN** (insep.): *(food, colloquial)* to polish off; to put away; to devour; *(crumple)* to crush; to crease; *(sneak away)* to slip away

**WEGDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to push away; to thrust aside

**ZERDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to crush; to squash; to mash; to crumple

**ZUDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to press shut; to push closed; to squeeze shut

**ZUSAMMENDRÜCKEN** (sep.): to compress; to squeeze together; to crush; to squash
