# FÜHLEN

**FÜHLEN:** to feel; to sense; to perceive; to experience; to be aware of; to touch; to grope

**ABFÜHLEN** (sep.): to feel; to palpate; to examine by touch

**ANFÜHLEN** (sep.): to feel; to feel like; to have a texture; *(reflexive: sich anfühlen)* to feel to the touch

**BEFÜHLEN** (insep.): to feel; to touch; to handle; to finger; to palpate

**EINFÜHLEN** (sep.): to empathize; to identify with; to feel one's way into; to put oneself in another's place

**ERFÜHLEN** (insep.): to sense; to feel out; to perceive through touch; to intuit

**HERAUSFÜHLEN** (sep.): to sense; to discern; to perceive; to detect

**MITFÜHLEN** (sep.): to sympathize; to empathize; to feel with; to commiserate; to share in another's feelings

**NACHFÜHLEN** (sep.): to empathize; to understand another's feelings; to relate to emotionally

**VORFÜHLEN** (sep.): to feel out; to sound out; to test the waters
