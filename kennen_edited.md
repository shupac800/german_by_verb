# KENNEN

**KENNEN:** to know; to be acquainted with; to be familiar with; to recognize

**AUSKENNEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich auskennen)* to know one's way around; to be well versed; to be knowledgeable

**BEKENNEN** (insep.): to confess; to avow; to profess; to declare; to acknowledge; to admit

**ERKENNEN** (insep.): *(identify)* to recognize; to identify; to discern; to make out; *(understand)* to realize; to perceive; to detect; *(legal)* to award; to impose

**HERAUSKENNEN** (sep.): to recognize from; to distinguish; to discern

**VERKENNEN** (insep.): to misjudge; to fail to appreciate; to underestimate; to misread
