# RINGEN

**RINGEN:** to wrestle; to struggle; to grapple; to contend

**ABRINGEN** (sep.): to wrest from; to extract by force; to wring from

**AUSRINGEN** (sep.): to wring out; to squeeze out

**BERINGEN** (insep.): to ring; to band; to put a ring on

**DURCHRINGEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich durchringen)* to bring oneself to; to come to a decision after struggling

**ERRINGEN** (insep.): to win; to achieve; to gain through effort

**UMRINGEN** (insep.): to surround; to encircle; to crowd around
