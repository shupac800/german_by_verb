# GRABEN

**GRABEN:** to dig; to excavate; to burrow; to delve; to spade; to mine

**ABGRABEN** (sep.): to dig away; to dig off; to excavate away

**ANGRABEN** (sep.): *(colloquial)* to flirt with; to chat up; to hit on

**AUFGRABEN** (sep.): to dig up; to excavate; to unearth

**AUSGRABEN** (sep.): to dig up; to excavate; to unearth; to exhume; to disinter

**BEGRABEN** (insep.): to bury; to inter; to entomb; to lay to rest; *(figurative)* to bury; to abandon; to let go of

**EINGRABEN** (sep.): to dig in; to bury; to entrench; to embed

**GEGRABEN** (adj.): dug; excavated; graven

**NACHGRABEN** (sep.): to dig again; to dig further; to dig deeper

**UMGRABEN** (sep.): to dig over; to turn over; to till; to dig up

**UNTERGRABEN** (insep.): to undermine; to sap; to subvert; to erode

**VERGRABEN** (insep.): to bury; to hide underground; to cache; to conceal
