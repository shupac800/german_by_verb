# DANKEN

**DANKEN:** to thank; to express gratitude; to show appreciation

**ABDANKEN** (sep.): to abdicate; to resign; to step down; to relinquish office or power

**BEDANKEN** (insep.): to thank; to express gratitude; *(reflexive: sich bedanken)* to say thank you

**VERDANKEN** (insep.): to owe; to be indebted to; to have to thank for
