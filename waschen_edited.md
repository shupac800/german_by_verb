# WASCHEN

**WASCHEN:** to wash; to clean; to launder; to scrub; to bathe; to rinse; to sluice

**ABWASCHEN** (sep.): to wash off; to wash up; to do the dishes

**AUFWASCHEN** (sep.): to wash up; to cleanse; to do the washing up

**AUSWASCHEN** (sep.): to wash out; to wash away; to rinse out; to erode; to scour; to wear away

**UNTERWASCHEN** (insep.): to undermine; to wash away from beneath; to erode underneath

**VERWASCHEN** (adj.): washed out; faded; blurred; vague; woolly; wishy-washy
