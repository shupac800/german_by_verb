# TRAUEN

**TRAUEN:** *(trust)* to trust; to rely on; to have confidence in; *(dare)* to dare; to venture; *(marry)* to marry; to perform a marriage ceremony

**BETRAUEN** (insep.): to entrust; to charge with; to commission

**GETRAUEN** (insep.): to dare; to venture

**HERTRAUEN** (sep.): to dare to come here; to venture near

**MISSTRAUEN** (insep.): to distrust; to mistrust; to be suspicious of; to doubt

**VERTRAUEN** (insep.): to trust; to trust in; to rely on; to have confidence in; to have faith in; to confide in

**ZUTRAUEN** (sep.): to believe capable of; to have faith in; to credit with
