# PASSEN

**PASSEN:** to fit; to suit; to match; to be suitable; to be convenient; to be appropriate

**ABPASSEN** (sep.): to wait for; to waylay; to catch at the right moment

**ANPASSEN** (sep.): to adapt; to adjust; to fit; to tailor; to conform; to match

**AUFPASSEN** (sep.): to watch out; to be careful; to pay attention; to look after; to keep an eye on

**EINPASSEN** (sep.): to fit in; to insert; to adjust to fit

**HINPASSEN** (sep.): to fit in; to belong; to be suitable

**VERPASSEN** (insep.): to miss; to let slip; to fail to catch

**ZUSAMMENPASSEN** (sep.): to go together; to match; to be compatible; to suit each other
