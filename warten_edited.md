# WARTEN

**WARTEN:** *(patience)* to wait; to await; to bide; to hold on; to hang about; *(maintenance)* to service; to maintain; to tend; to look after; to keep up

**ABWARTEN** (sep.): to wait for; to await; to wait and see; to bide one's time; to temporize; to hold off

**AUFWARTEN** (sep.): to wait on; to serve; to attend to; to call upon

**ERWARTEN** (insep.): to expect; to anticipate; to await; to count on; to look forward to

**ZUWARTEN** (sep.): to wait patiently; to bide one's time
