# WERFEN

**WERFEN:** to throw; to cast; to hurl; to toss; to fling; to pitch; to sling; *(animals)* to give birth to; to whelp

**ABWERFEN** (sep.): *(shed)* to shed; to moult; to throw off; to slough; to cast off; *(unseat)* to unseat; to throw from the saddle; *(yield)* to yield; to produce a return; *(drop)* to drop; to toss down

**ANWERFEN** (sep.): to start up; to set in motion; to crank

**AUFWERFEN** (sep.): *(raise)* to raise; to bring up; to pose; to give rise to; *(pile)* to pile up; to throw up; to fling up

**AUSWERFEN** (sep.): to throw out; to eject; to cast out; to expel; to spit out; to disgorge; to expectorate

**BEWERFEN** (insep.): to pelt; to throw at; to plaster; to render

**EINWERFEN** (sep.): *(break)* to smash; to shatter; to break; *(insert)* to insert; to throw in; to drop in; to post; to mail; *(interject)* to interject; to interpose

**ENTGEGENWERFEN** (sep.): to fling at; to hurl at; to throw in the face of

**ENTWERFEN** (insep.): to design; to draft; to sketch; to outline; to devise; to plan; to draw up; to conceptualize; to lay out; to create

**FORTWERFEN** (sep.): to throw away; to discard; to fling away

**HERAUSWERFEN** (sep.): to throw out; to eject; to expel

**HINWERFEN** (sep.): to throw down; to fling down; to toss off; to dash off

**UMWERFEN** (sep.): *(knock over)* to knock over; to overturn; to topple; to tip over; *(astound)* to bowl over; to stun; to overwhelm

**UNTERWERFEN** (insep.): to subject; to subjugate; to subdue; to conquer; to enslave; *(reflexive)* to submit; to yield

**VERWERFEN** (insep.): to reject; to discard; to dismiss; to quash; to overrule; to condemn; to repudiate; to turn down; to set aside

**VORWERFEN** (sep.): to reproach; to blame; to accuse of; to hold against

**WEGWERFEN** (sep.): to throw away; to discard; to toss away; to cast away; to jettison; to dump; to trash; to scrap

**ZURÜCKWERFEN** (sep.): *(throw back)* to throw back; *(repel)* to repel; to drive back; *(reflect)* to reflect; to reverberate; *(setback)* to set back

**ZUSAMMENWERFEN** (sep.): to throw together; to jumble; to lump together
