# KOCHEN

**KOCHEN:** to cook; to boil; to prepare food; to seethe

**ABKOCHEN** (sep.): to boil; to sterilize by boiling

**ANKOCHEN** (sep.): to parboil; to bring to a boil

**AUFKOCHEN** (sep.): to bring to a boil; to boil up

**AUSKOCHEN** (sep.): to boil out; to extract by boiling; to sterilize by boiling

**EINKOCHEN** (sep.): to boil down; to reduce; to preserve; to can; to bottle

**VERKOCHEN** (insep.): to overcook; to boil away; to cook to nothing

**VORKOCHEN** (sep.): to precook; to cook in advance; to prepare ahead

**ÜBERKOCHEN** (insep.): to boil over; to overflow
