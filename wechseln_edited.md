# WECHSELN

**WECHSELN:** to change; to alter; to exchange; to replace; to substitute; to switch; to alternate; to vary; to swap; to take turns

**ABWECHSELN** (sep.): to alternate; to take turns; to rotate; to interchange

**AUSWECHSELN** (sep.): to exchange; to replace; to substitute; to swap; to trade

**EINWECHSELN** (sep.): to change money; to cash; to cash in

**UMWECHSELN** (sep.): to change; to exchange; to convert; to swap

**VERWECHSELN** (insep.): to confuse; to mix up; to mistake one for another; to muddle; to confound
