# WECKEN

**WECKEN:** to wake; to awaken; to rouse; to arouse; to wake up

**AUFWECKEN** (sep.): to wake up; to awaken; to rouse; to stir

**EINWECKEN** (sep.): *(food preservation)* to preserve; to can; to jar; to bottle

**ERWECKEN** (insep.): *(figurative)* to awaken; to arouse; to inspire; to stir up; to call forth; to excite; to animate; to trigger
