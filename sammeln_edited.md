# SAMMELN

**SAMMELN:** to collect; to gather; to accumulate; to assemble

**ANSAMMELN** (sep.): to accumulate; to amass; to build up; to stockpile

**AUFSAMMELN** (sep.): to pick up; to gather up; to collect

**EINSAMMELN** (sep.): to collect; to gather in; to take in

**VERSAMMELN** (insep.): to assemble; to gather; to convene; to rally

**ZUSAMMENSAMMELN** (sep.): to gather together; to collect together
