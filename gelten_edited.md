# GELTEN

**GELTEN:** to be valid; to apply; to hold; to count; to be in effect; to prevail; to be considered; to stand

**ABGELTEN** (sep.): to compensate; to settle; to remunerate; to satisfy; to pay off

**ENTGELTEN** (insep.): to pay for; to compensate; to reimburse; to remunerate

**VERGELTEN** (insep.): to repay; to requite; to retaliate; to avenge; to reciprocate; to pay back
