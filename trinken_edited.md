# TRINKEN

**TRINKEN:** to drink; to imbibe; to consume beverages; to carouse

**ANTRINKEN** (sep.): *(begin drinking)* to start drinking; *(get tipsy)* sich einen antrinken — to get tipsy; to get a buzz on

**AUSTRINKEN** (sep.): to drink up; to drain; to finish one's drink; to empty one's glass

**BETRINKEN** (insep.): sich betrinken — to get drunk; to become intoxicated; to get sloshed

**ERTRINKEN** (insep.): to drown; to be drowned; to perish in water
