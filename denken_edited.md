# DENKEN

**DENKEN:** to think; to reason; to consider; to conceive; to assume; to imagine

**AUSDENKEN** (sep.): to think up; to devise; to invent; to conceive; to concoct; to contrive; to imagine

**BEDENKEN** (insep.): to consider; to think over; to contemplate; to ponder; to take into account; to bear in mind; to reflect on

**DURCHDENKEN** (insep.): to think through; to consider thoroughly; to reason through to a conclusion

**ERDENKEN** (insep.): to think up; to devise; to invent; to conceive; to contrive; to imagine into being

**GEDENKEN** (insep.): *(remember)* to commemorate; to memorialize; to honor the memory of; *(intend)* to intend; to mean to; to plan to

**HINZUDENKEN** (sep.): to add in one's mind; to mentally supplement; to imagine in addition

**NACHDENKEN** (sep.): to reflect; to ponder; to contemplate; to meditate; to ruminate; to think about carefully; to deliberate

**UMDENKEN** (sep.): to rethink; to reconsider; to change one's way of thinking; to shift perspective

**VERDENKEN** (insep.): to blame; to hold against; to reproach for

**ZURÜCKDENKEN** (sep.): to think back; to cast one's mind back; to recall

**ÜBERDENKEN** (insep.): to reconsider; to rethink; to review; to reassess; to think over again
