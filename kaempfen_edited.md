# KÄMPFEN

**KÄMPFEN:** to fight; to battle; to struggle; to combat; to compete; to contend; to wrestle; to campaign; to brawl

**ANKÄMPFEN** (sep.): to fight against; to struggle against; to battle against; to combat

**AUSKÄMPFEN** (sep.): to fight out; to battle to a conclusion; to settle by fighting

**BEKÄMPFEN** (insep.): to combat; to fight; to oppose; to battle; to struggle against; to fight against

**DURCHKÄMPFEN** (sep.): *(force through)* to fight one's way through; to push through; to force through; *(reflexive: sich durchkämpfen)* to struggle through; to fight one's way

**ERKÄMPFEN** (insep.): to win through struggle; to fight for and achieve; to gain by fighting

**HERAUSKÄMPFEN** (sep.): to fight one's way out; to win by fighting

**MITKÄMPFEN** (sep.): to fight alongside; to join in the fight; to take part in combat
