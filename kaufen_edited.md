# KAUFEN

**KAUFEN:** to buy; to purchase; to acquire

**ABKAUFEN** (sep.): to buy from; to purchase from

**ANKAUFEN** (sep.): to purchase; to buy; to acquire

**AUFKAUFEN** (sep.): to buy up; to buy out

**AUSKAUFEN** (sep.): to buy out; to buy up the entire stock

**EINKAUFEN** (sep.): to shop; to buy; to purchase; to do the shopping; to stock up

**ERKAUFEN** (insep.): to buy; to purchase at a cost; to obtain at a price

**LOSKAUFEN** (sep.): to ransom; to buy someone's freedom; to redeem

**VERKAUFEN** (insep.): to sell; to market; to vend

**ZUKAUFEN** (sep.): to buy in addition; to purchase additionally

**ZURÜCKKAUFEN** (sep.): to buy back; to repurchase; to reacquire
