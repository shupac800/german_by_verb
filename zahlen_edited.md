# ZAHLEN

**ZAHLEN:** to pay; to settle; to shell out; to give money for goods or to cancel debts

**ABZAHLEN** (sep.): to pay off; to pay back; to amortize; to pay for; to give what is due for

**ANZAHLEN** (sep.): to pay on account; to pay a deposit; to deposit

**AUSZAHLEN** (sep.): to pay out; to disburse; to pay off; to pay a debt in full; to buy out; to pay in return for forfeiting rights

**BEZAHLEN** (insep.): to pay; to defray; to cover the expenses of; to meet expenses; to settle; to remunerate; to pay off; to disburse

**EINZAHLEN** (sep.): to deposit; to pay in; to lodge

**NACHZAHLEN** (sep.): to pay extra; to pay later; to make up a payment

**ZURÜCKZAHLEN** (sep.): to repay; to pay back; to reimburse; to refund; to recoup; to pay off

**ZUSAMMENZAHLEN** (sep.): to add up; to total; to tally up; to tot up; to sum up; to figure up

**ZUZAHLEN** (sep.): to pay extra; to pay in addition

**ÜBERZAHLEN** (insep.): to overpay
