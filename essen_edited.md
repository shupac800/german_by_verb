# ESSEN

**ESSEN:** to eat; to consume food; to dine; to feed

**AUFESSEN** (sep.): to eat up; to finish eating; to clean one's plate; to finish off

**AUSESSEN** (sep.): to finish eating; to eat to the end; *(figurative)* to face the consequences

**VOLLESSEN** (sep.): to eat one's fill; to gorge; to stuff oneself

**ÜBERESSEN** (insep.): to overeat; to eat too much; to gorge; to binge
