# WEIHEN

**WEIHEN:** to consecrate; to sanctify; to bless; to ordain; to inaugurate; to dedicate; to devote; to hallow; to solemnize

**EINWEIHEN** (sep.): *(ceremonial)* to inaugurate; to open; to christen; to dedicate; *(initiate)* to initiate; to induct; to introduce; to let in on a secret

**ENTWEIHEN** (insep.): to desecrate; to profane; to defile; to commit sacrilege
