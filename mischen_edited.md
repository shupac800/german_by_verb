# MISCHEN

**MISCHEN:** to mix; to blend; to mingle; to shuffle

**AUFMISCHEN** (sep.): *(colloquial)* to stir up; to shake up; to cause a commotion

**BEIMISCHEN** (sep.): to add to the mix; to admix; to blend in

**DURCHMISCHEN** (insep.): to mix thoroughly; to blend completely; to intermingle

**EINMISCHEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich einmischen)* to interfere; to meddle; to butt in; to get involved

**MITMISCHEN** (sep.): to be involved; to have a hand in; to be mixed up in

**VERMISCHEN** (insep.): to mix; to blend; to merge; to mingle; to intermingle

**ZUSAMMENMISCHEN** (sep.): to mix together; to blend together; to combine
