# ZEICHNEN

**ZEICHNEN:** to draw; to sketch; to draft; to depict; to portray; to chart; to sign

**ABZEICHNEN** (sep.): to copy; to initial; *(emerge)* to loom; to become visible; to take shape

**ANZEICHNEN** (sep.): to mark; to indicate; to note; to draw on

**AUFZEICHNEN** (sep.): to record; to write down; to log; to tape; to set down; to plot; to inscribe

**AUSZEICHNEN** (sep.): *(honor)* to honor; to decorate; to cite; to distinguish; *(pricing)* to price; to label; to mark; to ticket

**BEZEICHNEN** (insep.): to designate; to denote; to indicate; to describe; to term; to signify; to mark; to call

**DURCHZEICHNEN** (sep.): to trace

**EINZEICHNEN** (sep.): to mark in; to draw in

**NACHZEICHNEN** (sep.): to copy; to trace; to duplicate

**UMZEICHNEN** (sep.): to redraw; to draw again

**UNTERZEICHNEN** (insep.): to sign; to underwrite; to subscribe

**VERZEICHNEN** (insep.): *(record)* to record; to register; to note; to index; to list; *(distort)* to misrepresent; to distort

**ZURÜCKZEICHNEN** (sep.): to redraw

**ÜBERZEICHNEN** (insep.): to oversubscribe
