# RUFEN

**RUFEN:** to call; to shout; to cry out; to summon

**ABRUFEN** (sep.): to call up; to retrieve; to recall; to fetch

**ANRUFEN** (sep.): *(telephone)* to call; to phone; to ring; *(appeal)* to appeal to; to invoke

**AUFRUFEN** (sep.): *(call upon)* to call upon; to summon; *(invoke)* to invoke; to call up

**AUSRUFEN** (sep.): to exclaim; to cry out; to proclaim; to declare

**BERUFEN** (insep.): to appoint; to nominate; to designate; to convene

**HERAUSRUFEN** (sep.): to call out; to shout out

**HERRUFEN** (sep.): to call over; to call here

**VERRUFEN** (adj.): disreputable; notorious; infamous; ill-reputed

**WIDERRUFEN** (insep.): to revoke; to retract; to rescind; to recant; to withdraw

**WIEDERRUFEN** (sep.): to call again

**ZURUFEN** (sep.): to shout to; to call out to

**ZURÜCKRUFEN** (sep.): *(telephone)* to call back; to return a call; *(recall)* to recall; to call back

**ZUSAMMENRUFEN** (sep.): to call together; to convene; to assemble; to summon

**ÜBERRUFEN** (insep.): to overbid
