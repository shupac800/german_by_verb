# SAGEN

**SAGEN:** to say; to tell; to state; to mean

**ABSAGEN** (sep.): to cancel; to call off; to decline

**ANSAGEN** (sep.): to announce; to declare

**AUFSAGEN** (sep.): to recite; to say from memory

**AUSSAGEN** (sep.): *(testify)* to testify; to give evidence; to make a statement; *(express)* to say; to express; to convey

**BESAGEN** (insep.): to mean; to signify; to imply; to state

**DURCHSAGEN** (sep.): to announce; to pass on a message

**ENTSAGEN** (insep.): to renounce; to give up; to forgo; to relinquish

**HERSAGEN** (sep.): to recite; to reel off; to rattle off

**LOSSAGEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich lossagen)* to renounce; to disavow; to break with

**NACHSAGEN** (sep.): *(repeat)* to repeat; *(attribute)* to say of someone; to attribute to

**UNTERSAGEN** (insep.): to forbid; to prohibit; to ban

**VERSAGEN** (insep.): *(fail)* to fail; to break down; to malfunction; *(deny)* to deny; to refuse

**VORSAGEN** (sep.): to recite; to say aloud; to prompt

**ZUSAGEN** (sep.): *(promise)* to promise; to assure; *(accept)* to accept; to confirm; *(appeal)* to appeal to; to suit
