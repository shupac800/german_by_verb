# FLIEGEN

**FLIEGEN:** to fly; to travel by air; to soar; to pilot; to navigate

**ABFLIEGEN** (sep.): to take off; to depart by air; to fly off; *(birds)* to migrate

**ANFLIEGEN** (sep.): to approach; to fly toward; to head for

**AUFFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly up; *(figurative)* to be exposed; to be uncovered; to blow up

**AUSFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly out; to fly away; to leave the nest

**BEFLIEGEN** (insep.): to fly over; to serve a route by air

**DURCHFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly through; to fly nonstop; *(fail)* to fail; to flunk

**EINFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly in; to test-fly; to break in a new aircraft

**ENTFLIEGEN** (insep.): to fly away; to escape; to flee

**FORTFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly away; to fly off

**HERAUSFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly out; *(colloquial)* to be thrown out; to be fired; to be expelled

**VERFLIEGEN** (insep.): to blow over; to pass; to vanish; to fade; to evaporate; to dissipate

**WEGFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly away; to fly off; to be blown away

**ZUFLIEGEN** (sep.): to fly toward; *(figurative)* to come easily to

**ÜBERFLIEGEN** (insep.): to fly over; to overfly; *(read)* to skim; to scan; to glance over
