# LESEN

**LESEN:** to read; to lecture; to pick; to gather; to harvest

**ABLESEN** (sep.): to read off; to read out; to read from a meter or gauge

**AUFLESEN** (sep.): to pick up; to gather up; to collect

**AUSLESEN** (sep.): *(select)* to pick out; to select; to sort; *(finish reading)* to finish reading; *(computing)* to read out; to retrieve

**BELESEN** (adj.): well-read; erudite; learned; scholarly

**DURCHLESEN** (sep.): to read through; to read from start to finish

**EINLESEN** (sep.): *(reflexive: sich einlesen)* to read one's way in; to familiarize oneself through reading; *(computing)* to read in; to import

**ERLESEN** (adj.): exquisite; select; choice; refined

**HERAUSLESEN** (sep.): to read out; to extract from a text; to glean

**NACHLESEN** (sep.): to look up; to read up on; to check a reference

**VERLESEN** (insep.): *(read aloud)* to read aloud; to read out; *(misread, reflexive)* to misread; *(sort)* to sort; to pick over

**VORLESEN** (sep.): to read aloud; to read out; to read to someone

**WIEDERLESEN** (sep.): to reread; to read again

**ZERLESEN** (adj.): well-thumbed; dog-eared; worn from reading
