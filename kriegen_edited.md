**HERAUSKRIEGEN** (sep.): to find out; to figure out; to work out; to solve

**HINKRIEGEN** (sep.): to manage; to pull off; to get right; to fix

**UNTERKRIEGEN** (sep.): to get down; to wear down; to demoralize

**ZURÜCKKRIEGEN** (sep.): to get back; to recover

**ZUSAMMENKRIEGEN** (sep.): to gather; to collect; to scrape together; to assemble
