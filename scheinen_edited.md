# SCHEINEN

**SCHEINEN:** *(shine)* to shine; to gleam; to glow; *(seem)* to seem; to appear; to look as if

**BESCHEINEN** (insep.): to shine on; to illuminate; to irradiate

**DURCHSCHEINEN** (sep.): to shine through; to show through; to be translucent

**ERSCHEINEN** (insep.): *(appear)* to appear; to show up; to turn up; *(be published)* to be published; to come out

**WIDERSCHEINEN** (sep.): to reflect; to be reflected
